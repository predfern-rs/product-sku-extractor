Convert old image URLs to the new versions hosted on our own domain. Pick the brand, paste the old URLs (one per line), and the tool rebuilds each under https://www.<domain>/images/…. Handles both patterns: imgix (ridestore.imgix.net/catalog/product/FILE.jpg → /images/FILE.jpg) and Cloudways WordPress (wordpress-1269845-4582243.cloudwaysapps.com/wp-content/uploads/… → /images/wordpress-4582243/uploads/…). Runs entirely in your browser - no page fetching.
